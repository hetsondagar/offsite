# Project Guidelines

## Site Risk Assessment Standards

### DPR Submission Requirements
- Daily Progress Reports (DPRs) must be submitted within 24 hours of work completion
- Delays exceeding 1 day indicate potential project management issues
- Consistent delays may signal resource constraints or communication gaps

### Attendance Standards
- Minimum attendance rate: 70% of assigned team members
- Attendance variance > 30% indicates potential workforce instability
- Regular attendance patterns are critical for project timeline adherence

### Material Management
- Material requests should be approved within 48 hours
- Pending approvals > 2 indicate bottleneck in approval workflow
- Material shortages directly impact construction progress and timeline

### Project Health Indicators
- Active projects should maintain > 60% task completion rate
- Projects in planning stage require closer monitoring
- Budget remaining < 20% requires immediate attention

## Risk Levels

**LOW (0-40 risk score):**
- All indicators within acceptable ranges
- No immediate action required
- Continue standard monitoring

**MEDIUM (41-70 risk score):**
- One or more indicators showing deviation
- Requires increased monitoring frequency
- Consider preventive measures

**HIGH (71-100 risk score):**
- Multiple indicators showing significant deviation
- Immediate intervention required
- Escalate to project management team
